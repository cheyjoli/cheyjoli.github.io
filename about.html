About Me

Hello, welcome to my portfolio site! My name is Hallie Amat, and I’m a 20-year-old student from Huntersville, NC, which is just north of Charlotte (that’s where Luke Maye is from)!

I’m a sophomore at UNC Chapel Hill studying Psychology and Management. A few of my favorite things include movies, art, my cats, and all things sweet. I hope you enjoy learning more about me through my site!

About My Art

I have always loved drawing. I didn’t start painting until senior year of high school, but once I started, I loved it. Art is my biggest passion, and I’m excited to share it with you all.

In my art, I love capturing people’s likeness and spirit in a still image. I developed my skills through art classes, and now I just pursue it on my own time as a hobby and way to relieve stress.
